A cute and Comfy Platformer: "Benny's world"

This was something on the light, cuter side. The main character’s name is Ben, and this musically based game is meant to be a platform game like Mario, where the character runs through this imaginary magic world getting several prizes like candy and toys. His goal is to get 5 special missing instruments to bring music back to the magical land.

Exploring and Building: 'Charlotte's World'

This game was an extension of Ben’s game, perhaps his sister having her own spin-off game. Except hers is not a platformer, but rather a 3d game with movement all around where in her imagination, she's a mayor. The game is heavily based on Animal Crossing, one of my favourite game series. As mayor, she (and the player) has several tasks to do such as

  1. Build public projects
    • Schools
    • Hospitals
  2. Interact and play with citizens
  3. Travel to different parts of the world
  4. And much more

Time Travel and Adventure: 'The Quinn Paradox'

This is a more mature game idea; not as cute and fluffy as the other few game ideas. The concept is based on a teenage guy who’s a physics and engineering student, who managed to accidently get a time machine to work, but unfortunately, he’s caused a major problem, like a rift between realities. It’s up to him (the player) and the alternate versions of himself to clean up his mess and stop the realities from colliding.

History with a Twist: 'Historia'

Historia is, well as the name suggests, a history-based game. I’m an avid history buff, and having a game based on some of my favourite time periods is something I’ve thought of doing. The game is an educational-adventure type of game, where you learn about certain events through the eyes of a minor character, get to meet some of the histories' greatest (and not-so-great) people, and learn the impact their actions had on our lives today. The picture beside is some concept art for Napoleon and Charles de Gaulle, just to plan out what the basic character would look like but I plan to add more characters and designs to the set.

Silliness and Fun: 'Bobble Wrap Gang'

This is less of a game, and more of an interactive comic. It’s based on 4 characters I created, each with dumb but lovable personalities, that mostly goof around but share valuable life lessons. I haven’t spent a lot of time developing this one, but I was thinking perhaps the player can create a character to join them and they’d go on dumb, silly and lighthearted adventures together. Let’s introduce each character:

  1. Bella Bunny - Level-headed leader who makes sure everyone hasn’t gone mad (yet)
  2. Fiona Frog - Has a Napoleon Complex and is very sassy, but she means well (most times)
  3. Delilah Dog - Might not be the brightest, but has the biggest heart
  4. Cecelia Cat - The smart one who would rather be buried in her books than anywhere else. Unfortunately, she’s stuck with these 3
