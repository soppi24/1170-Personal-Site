Meeting Sophie Eruokwu

Hello there! My name is Sophie Eruokwu, and this site showcases some of my work. I mainly focus on the realm of design, but my goal is to be a game designer and bring my character and concept designs to life in terms of games. I’ve been drawing since I was about 7 or so, and I've been constantly improving as I grew up. I learned that I had a great interest in design, especially character design and concept art.


Architectural Designer Creates a Pixel Art Town. Retrieved February 9, 2024 from https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMJbhee4xTk

I have dabbled in other areas of art such as photography, clay creations, embroidery, writing and much more. Additionally, I’m the author of two children's books, that I also illustrated. In terms of my art, I tend to do traditional, old-fashioned paper and pencil art, and sometimes watercolour (my best medium) but I also use digital art sometimes as it's more convenient and easier to edit and share.

The video beside was one of my biggest inpirations to get into game development. I had intally wanted to get into architecture and design, but I relised I could combine by ability to design, with my abilty to tell stories, and create games as an output (not just books).
